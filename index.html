About Me

Hi my name is Rubi Lara, I'm a dynamic professional with a passion for entrepreneurship and customer-centricity. With a diverse background spanning customer service, e-commerce, and retail management I bring a unique blend of skills to every endeavor. I have a passion for photography and digital design. From editing photos to creating content for websites, I love bringing ideas to life through visual mediums. My portfolio showcases a mix of my shop photos, product shots, and edited images that capture the essence of my style and aesthetic.


From designing and creating unique Swarovski tooth gems to managing inventory and shipping logistics, I've honed my ecommerce roles while delivering exceptional customer experiences. Through effective communication and a keen focus on customer satisfaction, I've built enduring relationships with both clients and suppliers.


I am constantly inspired by the world around me and enjoy experimenting with different techniques to push the boundaries of what's possible. Armed with a Bachelor's degree in Marketing from California State University, Long Beach, I'm eager to continue exploring the intersection of creativity and commerce, always striving to innovate and delight customers along the way.

Thank you for taking the time to learn about me. I can't wait to embark on our next creative journey together!
